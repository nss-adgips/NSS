Dear NSS ADGIPS Family and Friends,

Welcome to the official website of the National Service Scheme (NSS) at Dr. Akhilesh Das Gupta Institiue of Professional Studies (ADGIPS). We are excited to share our journey of service, leadership, and community engagement with you. At NSS ADGIPS, our mission is to empower students to make a meaningful impact in society, embracing our motto, "Not Me, But You." This guiding principle fuels our commitment to volunteerism and inspires us to contribute positively to our communities. Whether you're an active volunteer, a prospective member, or someone simply interested in learning more about our work, we invite you to explore our website. Here, you'll find information about our projects, upcoming events, and countless opportunities to get involved. Join us as we work together to create positive change, grow as leaders, and inspire one another.
